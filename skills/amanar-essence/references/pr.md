# Essence — PR and issue text

The reader is a reviewer or a future maintainer, not a participant in the
conversation that produced the change.

- Lead with the problem and the fix. Nothing else is required.
- Cut: "as discussed", step-by-step narration of how you got here, restatement
  of the ticket, and closing recaps.
- Keep: the concrete behavior change, the risk and how it was mitigated, how to
  verify, and any follow-up deliberately deferred.
- Repro steps, commands, and error strings verbatim.
- A short PR needs no headings. Add them only when length genuinely benefits.
