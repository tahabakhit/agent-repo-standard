# Amanar Inquire

Inspect available context first. Separate verified facts, assumptions, preferences,
constraints, and unresolved decisions. Ask only questions whose answers materially
change scope, architecture, cost, authority, risk, or outcome, one focused question
at a time with a recommendation.

Produce a concise success contract covering purpose, actors, objective, scope,
non-goals, authority boundaries, acceptance criteria, evidence, risks, and open
blockers. Research only named uncertainties. Do not smuggle implementation choices
into requirements or treat a preferred solution as the problem statement.
